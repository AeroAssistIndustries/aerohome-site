The Response Ladder

From first sound to all-clear,
in six steps.

Detect

A sensor, sound, or camera detection — a person at the fence at 2 a.m. — creates a candidate event.

Investigate

Sentinel launches to the exact zone. Guardian rolls to the room. Eyes on target in seconds.

Verify

On-device AI confirms person, vehicle, animal, or package. Raccoons never wake you. People do.

Deter

Spotlight locks on. A voice challenge announces police contact. Most intruders abandon the attempt right here.

Escalate

Live verified video to you and our 24/7 monitoring agents. One-tap 911 with your address and a live-feed link.

Document

Multi-angle 4K and thermal record, auto-packaged for police and your insurance claim.

Your footage.
Your rules. Period.
